Being an organization that is dedicated to bridging the gap between technologically less developed animal shelters and the broader community of potential adopters, donors, and volunteers. Recognizing that many smaller or rural shelters lack the resources or expertise to maintain a digital presence, PetConnect provides these shelters with tools and platforms to showcase their animals, connect with supporters, and receive much-needed donations.
