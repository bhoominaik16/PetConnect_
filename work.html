Adoptions

PetConnect plays the role of taking the adopters to the animal shelters helping the innocents find their new home. Due to lack of social media awareness, many animal shelters with best facilities don't get chance to showcase themselves infont of world and as its effect it gets difficult for those animals to find a loving family.
Till the date, we are responsible for 50+ adoptions.

Adopt Now!
Rescue

One of the major tasks, we manage to gather the information of injured animals who need emergency treatment through our Emergency Rescue Feature and share to the nearest animal shelter with the best facilities. The recued animals are given a proper treatment by expertise Vets and their complete care is taken.
We have got success in saving 26 innocent lives.
We urge you to use our feature to help the pure souls get a new life.
